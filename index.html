About me

PhD student in the MIND team (Inria) and the Brain&AI team (Meta FAIR Paris), supervised by Stéphane d’Ascoli and Thomas Moreau, working closely with the Brain & AI group led by Jean-Rémi King.

I build AI models to understand and decode the brain, with a focus on non-invasive communication decoding. PhD defense planned for January 2028. Previously: research intern at Meta, Institut Pasteur, and the New York Genome Center.

Passionate about culture générale. Once on Question pour un Champion 🎯.
